Why a Responsive Navigation Bar?

A responsive navigation bar is essential for modern websites and portfolios. It ensures that your site remains user-friendly and accessible across a wide range of devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones. By adapting to different screen sizes, a responsive navigation bar guarantees a seamless browsing experience for your visitors, regardless of the device they're using.
